Penny Black (United Kingdom)

The Penny Black, issued in 1840, is often considered the world's first adhesive postage stamp. It features a profile of Queen Victoria.

Usage: It was used for mailing letters within the United Kingdom.

Price: The cost was one penny, regardless of the distance traveled.
